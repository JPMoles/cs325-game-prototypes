  • Mandated Variety: I used input with the mouse. The placement of all physics bodies is deterministic for each play of the game. The playstyle is Performer. The genre is overhead arcade.
  • Prototype Goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating? This game is evaluating bouncing through mouse input.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game? I want my players to feel enjoyment in trying to direct the oasis in different ways to make it to the desert.
  • Gameplay: The player can direct the movement of the oasis by pressing the "left mouse" button. The player will bounce as it collides with the pins.
  • Strategies: I expect the player will try direct the oasis along a simple path to bounce to the desert.
