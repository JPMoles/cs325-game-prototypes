Playful Potions

  • High Concept: Jump to the skies to collect the falling potions and beat your high-score!
  • Theme: I used the chemist theme.
  • Mandated Variety: I used direct input with the keyboard. It has a little bit of randomness with the potion and bomb spawning. The playstyle is Competitor as one seeks to achieve a higher score on each run of the game. The genre is platformer.
  • Prototype Goal: What game mechanic is this prototype evaluating? This game is evaluating how basic jumping works with gravity and collisions with gameobjects help provide an interactive game.
  • Player Experience Goals: What experience do you want players to have when playing your game? I want my players to feel enjoyment when beating their high-scores and be distraight when they inevitably hit a bomb.
  • Gameplay: The player can jump by pressing the "up" button on their keyboard. They can move left with the "left" arrow key and "right" with the right arrow key. The player gains 10 points when collecting the green potions, and loses 12 points when collecting the red potions. When all the green potions have been collect more will spawn. This is the same with the red potions. When all the green potions have been collect a bomb will also spawn. Upon collision with the bomb the game ends the final score is shown in the top left.
  • Strategies: I expect the player will try to find jumping patterns to avoid the red potions and only collect the green potions. Also, watching for the bounce patterns of the bomb to avoid it at all cost.
